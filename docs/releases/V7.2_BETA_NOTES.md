## What Changed

V7.2 implements **3 critical filters** designed to eliminate the most common false signal patterns:

### 1. ‚≠ê Require Strong BOS Filter
**Problem:** Entry zones created after weak (W) BOS signals often fail because the initial impulse lacked conviction.

**Solution:**
- New toggle: `Require Strong BOS?` (default: ON)
- When enabled: Golden zones only appear after Strong (S) BOS
- Weak BOS will show BOS label, but NO entry zone

**Code Location:** Lines 1248-1249, 1280-1281

**Expected Impact:**
- Reduces total entry signals by ~30-40%
- Eliminates entries from weak/choppy impulse moves
- Significantly improves win rate on remaining signals

**Example:**
```
Before: BOS (W) ‚Üí Golden Zone appears ‚Üí Entry signal ‚Üí Often fails
After:  BOS (W) ‚Üí No golden zone ‚Üí No entry (avoided losing trade)
        BOS (S) ‚Üí Golden Zone appears ‚Üí Entry signal ‚Üí Higher probability
```

---

### 2. ‚≠ê Swing Respect Filter
**Problem:** If price touches the protected level during pullback, structure integrity is compromised. Entries after this often fail.

**Solution:**
- New toggle: `Require Swing Respect?` (default: ON)
- Tracks if LOW touches protected low (bull) or HIGH touches protected high (bear) during pullback
- Entry signal blocked if protected level was tested

**Code Location:** Lines 1362-1363, 1372-1373, 1430, 1435-1445

**State Variable:** `protectedLevelTested` (resets on new BOS)

**Expected Impact:**
- Catches structural weakness early
- Prevents entries on failed pullbacks
- Critical for avoiding "trap" setups

**Example - Bullish:**
```
BOS at 100 ‚Üí Protected Low = 95
Pullback: High 105 ‚Üí Low 98 (golden zone) ‚Üí ENTRY OK
Pullback: High 105 ‚Üí Low 94.5 (touched protected) ‚Üí NO ENTRY (structure compromised)
```

---

### 3. ‚≠ê Max Bars for Entry Filter
**Problem:** Stale signals - golden zones remain active indefinitely. Late entries have poor risk/reward.

**Solution:**
- New input: `Max Bars for Entry` (default: 25 bars)
- Tracks bars since BOS: `barsSincePullbackStart = bar_index - pullbackStartBar`
- Zone expires if no entry within limit

**Code Location:** Lines 1312-1313, 1325

**Expected Impact:**
- Prevents chasing old setups
- Forces entries near structure (better R:R)
- Auto-invalidates zones that take too long to develop

**Example (15m timeframe, 25 bars = 6.25 hours):**
```
BOS at 10:00 AM
Golden zone appears
15:00 PM (20 bars): Zone still valid, watching for entry
17:00 PM (28 bars): Zone expired, no longer monitoring
```

## Known Behaviors

### When Strong BOS Filter Blocks Zone
- BOS label still appears (with S or W suffix)
- NO golden zone box drawn
- Dashboard shows structure state, but NO entry status
- User sees: "BOS detected, but not strong enough for entry setup"

### When Swing Respect Catches Violation
- Golden zone IS visible
- Dashboard shows "ZONE‚ñ≤" or "READY‚ñ≤"
- But confirmation will NEVER trigger (blocked by filter)
- User sees: "Setup formed, but structure compromised"

### When Max Bars Expires Zone
- Golden zone remains visible (historical reference)
- `pullbackDirection` resets to 0
- New BOS required for new zone
- Dashboard shows structure, but no active entry monitoring
